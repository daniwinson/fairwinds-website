This Privacy Policy describes how Fairwinds ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information in connection with the M/Y PATRON maintenance management application and our related SMS text-messaging service (collectively, the "Service"). By using the Service, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the information needed to operate the Service, which may include:

  • Contact information — your name and mobile phone number, provided when you are added to the crew directory or when you opt in to text messaging.
  • Message content — the text of SMS messages you send to or receive from us, including maintenance trouble tickets created by text.
  • Operational records — equipment, maintenance, service-log, and parts data you enter into the application.
  • Technical data — basic log information (such as timestamps) generated when you use the Service.

2. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create, route, and track vessel maintenance tickets and alerts;
  • Send you SMS notifications related to maintenance activity, service reminders, and operational updates;
  • Maintain accurate equipment, service, and crew records; and
  • Operate, secure, and improve the Service.

3. SMS / Text Messaging Program

When you opt in, we may send you SMS messages relating to vessel maintenance, including ticket confirmations, status updates, service reminders, and operational alerts. Message frequency varies.

Message and data rates may apply.

  • To stop receiving messages at any time, reply STOP. You will receive a confirmation and no further messages unless you opt in again.
  • For help, reply HELP or contact us at the address below.
  • Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages.
No mobile information will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. Text messaging originator opt-in data and consent will not be shared with any third parties. Information may be shared only with subcontractors that provide support services to us (such as our SMS provider) strictly to deliver the Service.

4. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share, rent, or sell mobile phone numbers or SMS opt-in data with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. We may share information only:

  • With service providers who help us operate the Service (for example, our messaging platform and hosting providers), under obligations to protect the information and use it only to provide their services to us;
  • When required by law, legal process, or to protect the rights, safety, or property of our users or others.

5. Data Retention & Security

We retain information for as long as needed to operate the Service and meet legal and operational requirements, then delete or anonymize it. We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to protect information, though no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

6. Your Choices

You may opt out of SMS messages at any time by replying STOP. You may also request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information by contacting us using the details below.
