Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 16, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Fairwinds, LLC ("Fairwinds," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you visit our website, contact us, request or book a charter, or otherwise interact with our services. It also covers our separate SMS text-messaging service for vessel maintenance, described in Part B below. By using our website or services, you agree to the practices described here.

Part A — Website & Charter Services

This part applies to visitors to fairwindsnewport.com and to guests and clients who inquire about or book charters, lessons, or consulting with us.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only what we need to respond to you and provide our services:

  • Information you give us — your name, email address, phone number, preferred dates, party size, vessel of interest, and any details you include when you submit our charter inquiry form, email us, or call.
  • Booking & payment information — details needed to arrange a charter. Payments are handled by our third-party payment processors; we do not store full card numbers on our website.
  • Automatically collected data — standard technical information such as your IP address, browser type, device, pages viewed, and timestamps, collected through your browser and analytics tools.
  • Cookies & similar technologies — used to operate the site, remember preferences, and understand site usage. See Section 3.

2. How We Use Information

  • Respond to inquiries and provide quotes, availability, and charter details;
  • Arrange, confirm, and manage charters, lessons, and consulting engagements;
  • Process payments and maintain business and tax records;
  • Send service-related communications about your booking;
  • Maintain, secure, analyze, and improve our website and services; and
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms & Conditions.

3. Cookies & Analytics

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies to function properly and to help us understand how visitors use the site. You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings, though some features may not work as intended if you do. We may use analytics providers that set their own cookies to report aggregate, non-identifying usage statistics.

4. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share it only:

  • With service providers who help us operate our business — for example, our website host, form-submission provider, analytics tools, and payment processors — under obligations to protect it and use it only to provide services to us;
  • With captains, crew, or vendors strictly as needed to deliver a charter or service you have requested;
  • When required by law, legal process, or to protect the rights, safety, or property of our guests, our company, or others; and
  • In connection with a business transfer, such as a merger or sale of assets, subject to this Policy.

5. Data Retention & Security

We retain information for as long as needed to provide our services and to meet legal, accounting, and operational requirements, then delete or anonymize it. We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to protect information, though no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

6. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information, and to opt out of certain uses. To make a request, contact us using the details in Section 9. We will not discriminate against you for exercising these rights.

7. Third-Party Links & Social Media

Our website may link to third-party sites and platforms (such as Instagram). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites; please review their policies separately.

8. Children's Privacy

Our website and services are intended for adults. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us information, please contact us and we will delete it.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your privacy rights, contact us at:

  • Fairwinds, LLC — Newport Beach, California
  • Phone: (949) 510-8682
  • Or use the charter inquiry form on our website.

Part B — SMS Text-Messaging Service (Yacht Maintenance Program)

This part applies specifically to our SMS text-messaging service used in connection with the M/Y PATRON maintenance management application (the "Service") for authorized crew, contractors, and management personnel.

B1. Information We Collect

  • Contact information — your name and mobile phone number, provided when you are added to the crew directory or when you opt in to text messaging.
  • Message content — the text of SMS messages you send to or receive from us, including maintenance trouble tickets created by text.
  • Operational records — equipment, maintenance, service-log, and parts data you enter into the application.
  • Technical data — basic log information (such as timestamps) generated when you use the Service.

B2. How We Use Information

  • Create, route, and track vessel maintenance tickets and alerts;
  • Send you SMS notifications related to maintenance activity, service reminders, and operational updates;
  • Maintain accurate equipment, service, and crew records; and
  • Operate, secure, and improve the Service.

B3. SMS / Text Messaging Program

When you opt in, we may send you SMS messages relating to vessel maintenance, including ticket confirmations, status updates, service reminders, and operational alerts. Message frequency varies.

Message and data rates may apply.

  • To stop receiving messages at any time, reply STOP. You will receive a confirmation and no further messages unless you opt in again.
  • For help, reply HELP or contact us at the details in Section 9.
  • Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages.

No mobile information will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. Text messaging originator opt-in data and consent will not be shared with any third parties. Information may be shared only with subcontractors that provide support services to us (such as our SMS provider) strictly to deliver the Service.

B4. Sharing, Retention & Your Choices

We do not sell your personal information and do not share, rent, or sell mobile phone numbers or SMS opt-in data with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. We may share information only with service providers who help us operate the Service, or when required by law. We retain information for as long as needed to operate the Service and meet legal and operational requirements, then delete or anonymize it. You may opt out of SMS messages at any time by replying STOP, and you may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information by contacting us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes take effect when posted on this page, and we will update the "Last updated" date above.
