Expert Technical Solutions

For Your Vessel

Technical expertise meets blue water experience — from a captain who has depended on these same systems thousands of miles from land.

Captain Dale Winson brings decades of hands-on boat ownership and technical expertise to help you optimize your vessel's systems. Whether you're upgrading to sustainable power, installing modern electronics, or integrating onboard connectivity, Dale delivers solutions designed for reliability on the water — not just at the dock.

With over 50,000 miles of offshore experience, extensive bluewater cruising in the South Pacific and along the Mexican coast, and a background operating charter vessels from 35 to 73 feet, Dale understands what systems truly work in real-world conditions. His Decades of hands-on experience managing his own vessels and client projects have given him a practical command of both the technical and logistical sides of marine systems.

Don't trust your vessel's critical systems to shore-based technicians who've never left the harbor.
