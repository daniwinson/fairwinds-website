Your Instructor

Captain Dale Winson

Your gateway to confident, capable sailing — from Newport Harbor to the open Pacific.

Whether you're dreaming of your first bareboat charter, planning a family cruising adventure, or looking to sharpen your coastal navigation skills, Captain Dale Winson brings the experience and teaching approach to get you there safely and confidently.

Dale's teaching draws from over 50,000 miles of real-world sailing — including 10,000 miles exploring the South Pacific, cruising Mexico's Sea of Cortez with his family, and competitive racing aboard championship-winning vessels. When you learn from Dale, you're learning from someone who has navigated challenging conditions, managed diverse vessels, and made countless real decisions far from shore.

With over 400 days captaining charter vessels from 35 to 73 feet, Dale understands exactly what sailors need to know. He builds skills progressively, safely, and enjoyably — whether you're seeking ASA certification or developing expertise for your own adventures.

Instruction Programs

Choose Your
Path

01
Perfect for beginners

Intro to Sailing

Your first time on the water under sail. We cover the essentials — points of sail, basic boat handling, safety, and the language of sailing — in a relaxed, pressure-free environment aboard PatriciaAnn. You'll leave with genuine confidence and a real feel for the boat.

  • Half-day or full-day session
  • No experience required
  • Up to 2 active students per session
  • Tacking, jibing, sail trim, safety basics
  • Preparation for ASA 101
02
For daysailors building confidence

Coastal Seamanship

Build the skills to take a vessel out on your own. Navigation, weather awareness, anchoring, docking under sail, night operations, and VHF communication — structured around real Newport coastal conditions with an instructor who has done it thousands of times.

  • Multi-day program recommended
  • Some prior sailing helpful, not required
  • Anchoring, docking, navigation, VHF
  • Preparation for ASA 103 / 104 / 114
  • Overnight option available
03
For sailors ready to go further

Offshore Passage Prep

For sailors who have the coastal fundamentals and want to extend their range — to Catalina, the Channel Islands, Mexico, or the Pacific. Passage planning, offshore weather, watchkeeping, and the realities of bluewater sailing from someone who has crossed oceans with his family.

  • Multi-day and overnight formats
  • Coastal sailing experience required
  • Catalina passage option
  • Weather routing and watch systems
  • Ocean crossing preparation
04
Any level · Any goal

Custom Instruction

Not every sailor fits a program. Spinnaker handling, single-handed techniques, heavy weather preparation, boat systems, seamanship for a specific passage — if you have a goal, Dale will design a session around it. Couples and small groups welcome.

  • Goal-specific, any skill level
  • Couples and small groups welcome
  • Flexible scheduling
  • Can combine with a charter day
