PatriciaAnn

47' Luxury Sailing Yacht · Newport Beach

47' Performance Sailing Yacht

PatriciaAnn

PatriciaAnn is a 47-foot performance sailing yacht and the heart of the Fairwinds fleet. Built for both bluewater passages and leisurely coastal sails, she carries a storied history — including offshore Pacific racing and a voyage to French Polynesia — and wears it beautifully.

Under sail, PatriciaAnn comes alive. Her tall rig and responsive helm make her a joy in any breeze, while her spacious cockpit and comfortable cabin ensure your guests are at ease from departure to return. Whether you're chasing the wind to Catalina or watching the Newport lights fade astern at sunset, she delivers the kind of sailing that stays with you.

Capacity is limited to six guests, ensuring a genuinely intimate experience. This is the charter for those who want to actually sail — not just motor out and back.
