Patron

73' Italian Luxury Motor Yacht · Newport Beach

73' Navetta by Absolute Yachts

Patron

Patron is a 73-foot Navetta 73 — an Italian-built motor yacht of extraordinary refinement. Designed by Absolute Yachts and crafted with the precision and aesthetic sensibility that defines the finest European yacht building, she is arguably the most impressive vessel in Newport Harbor.

Aboard Patron, the experience is effortlessly luxurious. Three decks offer a range of social spaces: the expansive aft cockpit for alfresco dining, the full-beam salon with panoramic glazing, a flybridge lounge above, and a bow sun deck ideal for Champagne and sea air. The galley is fully equipped for a private chef to work in comfort.

She accommodates up to twelve guests on day charters and is the natural choice for milestone celebrations, corporate entertaining, and any occasion that calls for something truly exceptional.
